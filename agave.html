SUMMARY

Agave, a dynamic real estate development startup rooted in Paraguay, is revolutionizing the industry by delivering exceptional quality buildings at affordable prices, specifically tailored to meet the needs of new and burgeoning young families. They asked me to tailor their message, look and feel.

ROLE

Brand Designer, Creative Director

CONCEPT

No items found.
We wanted to convey a strong positive message to hard-working families. The real estate market is getting increasingly harsh upon young families as gentrification trends between wealthy developers, making housing expensive and ridiculously exclusive. We wanted to bring joy and a spark of hope to those fighting to make the dream house a reality. Real photos, real feelings, real estate for all.
